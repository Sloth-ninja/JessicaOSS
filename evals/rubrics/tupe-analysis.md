# Rubric — TUPE Analysis

Score each dimension 1–5; the overall score may not exceed `no_invented_facts`.

## issue_spotting_completeness

The analysis must address: whether a relevant transfer occurs (both the business
transfer and service provision change limbs), which employees transfer, dismissal
protection, information and consultation duties, any measures envisaged, and a
concluding action list. 5 = all present and correctly reasoned from the facts
given; deduct for each omitted element.

## correct_law

Conclusions must be accurate under the Transfer of Undertakings (Protection of
Employment) Regulations 2006 (SI 2006/246): reg 3 (relevant transfer — business
transfer / service provision change), reg 4 (automatic transfer of contracts of
employment and liabilities), reg 7 (dismissal connected with the transfer is
automatically unfair unless for an ETO reason entailing changes in the
workforce), and regs 13–15 (information and, where measures are envisaged,
consultation duties). Any reference must cite the specific regulation number.
Wrong or vague law caps this at 2.

## uk_terminology

UK English and UK employment-law terminology throughout ("transferor"/
"transferee", "relevant transfer", "organised grouping", not US "successor
employer" language). Any US terminology from the CLAUDE.md table caps this at 3.

## no_invented_facts

Every factual claim (which employees are assigned to the grouping, dates,
transaction structure) must be traceable to the source document(s) supplied with
the output. Any fact not present in the source is an invented fact: score 1 and
list each in `failures`.
