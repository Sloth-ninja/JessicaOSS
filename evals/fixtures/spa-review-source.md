## 9. LIMITATIONS ON LIABILITY (SCHEDULE 5 SUMMARY TERMS)

The following limitations, set out in full in Schedule 5, apply to claims under the Warranties and the Tax Covenant:

9.1 **Aggregate cap.** The aggregate liability of the Sellers: (a) for all claims under the Business Warranties shall not exceed £12,750,000 (being 30 per cent. of the Headline Consideration); and (b) for all claims under this Agreement (including under the Title and Capacity Warranties, the Tax Warranties, the Tax Covenant and the Specific Indemnities) shall not exceed an amount equal to the Consideration actually received by the Sellers.

9.2 **De minimis.** The Sellers shall not be liable for any individual claim under the Business Warranties or the Tax Warranties unless the amount of that claim exceeds £25,000 (claims arising from the same or substantially similar facts being aggregated as a single claim for this purpose).

9.3 **Basket.** The Sellers shall not be liable for any claim under the Business Warranties or the Tax Warranties unless the aggregate amount of all such claims (excluding claims below the de minimis in clause 9.2) exceeds £425,000 (being 1 per cent. of the Headline Consideration), in which case the Sellers shall be liable for the whole amount of such claims and not only the excess.

9.4 **Time limits.** The Sellers shall not be liable for a claim unless written notice of the claim (setting out reasonable detail of the claim and, so far as practicable, the amount claimed) is given:

(a) in the case of a claim under the Business Warranties, within 18 months after the Completion Date;

(b) in the case of a claim under the Tax Warranties or the Tax Covenant, on or before the seventh anniversary of the Completion Date; and

(c) in the case of a claim under the Title and Capacity Warranties, on or before the sixth anniversary of the Completion Date.

9.5 Any claim notified within the applicable period shall (if not previously satisfied, settled or withdrawn) be deemed withdrawn unless legal proceedings in respect of it are commenced and served within nine months of the date of notification.

9.6 None of the limitations in this clause 9 or Schedule 5 shall apply to any claim to the extent it arises from fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation by a Seller.

9.7 The de minimis in clause 9.2 and the basket in clause 9.3 do not apply to claims under the Title and Capacity Warranties, the Tax Covenant or the Specific Indemnities.

9.8 The Buyer may set off against the Deferred Consideration the amount of any claim under this Agreement which has been agreed in writing by the Sellers' Representative or finally determined by a court of competent jurisdiction before the due date for payment of the Deferred Consideration.
